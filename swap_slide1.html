Problem & Market Opportunity

🚫 The Problem

  • Existing platforms (Etsy, Amazon Handmade) are oversaturated with high fees
  • No dedicated peer-to-peer exchange / barter mechanism for artisans
  • Generic platforms lack meaningful creator identity & storytelling
  • Creators are isolated — no collaborative space for co-creation

📈 Market Opportunity

  • Global handmade crafts market: $739.95B (2024) → $983B by 2030
  • CAGR: 4.9% (2025–2030)
  • Online craft marketplace: $15B, growing at 12%/yr
  • No competitor offers integrated exchange + community features

Project Scope

✅ In Scope

  • Web-based marketplace (buy, sell, list handmade crafts)
  • Peer-to-peer exchange / swap system
  • Creator profiles (portfolio, bio, story)
  • Secure payment gateway (card, PayPal, wallets)
  • Order management & real-time tracking
  • Review & rating system
  • Community forums & direct messaging
  • Custom order & commission requests
  • Admin dashboard & moderation tools
  • Search, filter, category browsing
  • Email notifications & alerts
  • Basic analytics dashboard for sellers
  • Customer support ticketing system

🚫 Out of Scope (Phase 2)

  • Native mobile app (iOS / Android)
  • Physical shipping or logistics management
  • Cryptocurrency / blockchain payments
  • AI-powered product recommendation engine
  • Multi-language / multi-currency support
  • Third-party seller API integrations

⚠️ Scope is locked at baseline. Any additions require formal change control approval.
