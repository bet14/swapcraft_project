Problem & Market Opportunity
Why the handmade craft market needs a new platform
!
Artisan Pain Points
  • Existing platforms (Etsy, Amazon Handmade) charge high fees: 9.75% – 15%
  • No dedicated platform for peer-to-peer craft/material exchange or barter
  • Generic profiles fail to tell the artisan story — a key buyer motivator
  • No collaborative space for commissions and co-creation
  • Small artisans economically excluded by high platform costs
$
Cost of Inaction
  • Global handmade market: $739.95B (2024) → $983B by 2030 (CAGR 4.9%)
  • Online craft marketplace: $15B in 2024, growing at 12% annually
  • A competitor could fill this niche before SwapCraft launches
★
SwapCraft Differentiators
  • Unique Exchange/Swap Mechanics — no competitor offers integrated trading
  • Lower Fees — 5% commission vs. 9.75–15% on major platforms
  • Community-First Design — forums, maker tools, creator spotlights
  • Seller Empowerment — governance model addressing Etsy seller migration
  • Handmade Integrity — strict verification, no mass-produced items
  • Circular Economy — swap model supports sustainability trends
Market Gap Summary

No existing platform combines e-commerce + peer-to-peer exchange + artisan community in one dedicated space. SwapCraft fills this gap with a differentiated, low-cost, community-driven model.

Solution Options & Recommendation
Three strategic options evaluated against project goals
Option A — Do Nothing
Maintain status quo on existing platforms
✔ No investment required
✔ Zero organizational risk
✘ Market opportunity permanently lost
✘ Artisan community needs unmet
✘ Competitors fill the gap first
Not Recommended
Option B — Basic MVP Site
Simple listing site, no exchange or community
✔ Lower development cost
✔ Faster time to market
✔ Easier to maintain
✘ Fails to differentiate from Etsy/Amazon
✘ Exchange/collaboration gap not addressed
✘ Low user retention & engagement
Partially Viable (Phase 1 only)
Option C — Full SwapCraft Platform
Comprehensive marketplace + exchange + community
✔ Fully addresses all artisan needs
✔ Strong differentiation from competitors
✔ Multiple sustainable revenue streams
✔ Builds loyal community ecosystem
✔ Scalable architecture for future growth
✘ Higher upfront cost (~$172K CAPEX)
✘ 9-month development timeline
Strongly Recommended
Decision Rationale

Option C maximizes long-term value, user retention, and competitive positioning. It is the only path to achieving break-even by Year 2 and building a defensible market position in the growing creator economy.

Risk Overview
Key risks across operational, security, regulatory and financial categories
🔴 High Priority Risks
Low initial user adoption — Insufficient marketplace liquidity at launch
Mitigation: Pre-registration campaign, founding artisan onboarding, referral incentives
HighHigh
Revenue model uncertainty — Weak monetization before product-market fit
Mitigation: Test multiple revenue streams, validate willingness to pay before scaling
HighHigh
High CAC vs. conversion rate — Paid acquisition costs exceed returns
Mitigation: Focus on organic growth, referral programs, community partnerships
HighHigh
Strong competition from Etsy/Amazon — Market share pressure
Mitigation: Differentiate via exchange model, lower fees, niche community positioning
HighMed
🟡 Medium & Security Risks
Scope creep — Feature additions beyond baseline
Mitigation: Agile delivery, strict sprint goals, formal approval for changes
HighMed
Key developer unavailability — Team capacity constraints
Mitigation: Cross-train roles, maintain freelance pipeline
MedHigh
Exchange system abuse — Fake listings, false claims
Mitigation: User verification, listing moderation, escrow controls
