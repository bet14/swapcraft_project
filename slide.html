Project Background & Market Opportunity

Why SwapCraft is needed

Current Problems

  • Independent artisans lack a dedicated digital platform built for handmade trading and exchange.
  • Existing platforms like Etsy and eBay focus mainly on monetary sales, not swapping.
  • High fees reduce artisan profitability.
  • Generic marketplaces do not highlight creator identity and story well.
  • There is little support for collaboration, commissions, and peer exchange.

Opportunity

  • Global handmade craft market estimated at $739.95B in 2024.
  • Projected to exceed $983B by 2030.
  • Online craft marketplace segment is growing strongly.
  • SwapCraft can differentiate through exchange mechanics, lower fees, and community-first design.
  • Supports the creator economy and circular economy trends.
Slide 4

Project Purpose & Strategic Value

Why this project matters

  • Empower small-scale artisans with fair, low-cost market access.
  • Foster a sustainable circular economy through swapping of products and raw materials.
  • Build an engaged community of makers and buyers.
  • Create a platform that combines e-commerce, exchange, and collaboration in one place.
  • Offer stronger differentiation than traditional handmade marketplaces.

Economic Value

Lower commissions, multiple revenue streams, long-term break-even target by Year 2.

Social Value

Supports micro-entrepreneurs, women artisans, and rural creators.

Platform Value

Brings marketplace, swap, messaging, reviews, and community features together.

Slide 7

Solution Options & Recommendation

Evaluation of strategic choices

Option A: Do Nothing

  • No investment required
  • No project risk
  • But market opportunity is lost
  • Artisan pain points remain unsolved

Option B: Basic Listing Website

  • Lower cost
  • Faster to launch
  • But weak differentiation
  • Does not solve exchange/community gap

Option C: Full SwapCraft Platform

  • Marketplace + exchange + community
  • Strong differentiation
  • Multiple revenue streams
  • Recommended option

Recommendation: Proceed with Option C because it best addresses user needs, supports long-term growth, creates competitive advantage, and aligns with the project charter and business case.

Project Scope

What is included and excluded in Phase 1

In Scope

  • Web-based marketplace
  • Exchange/swap system
  • Creator profiles
  • Secure payment gateway
  • Order management and tracking
  • Review and rating system
  • Community forums and messaging
  • Custom orders / commissions
  • Admin dashboard
  • Search, filters, category browsing
  • Seller analytics dashboard
  • Customer support ticketing

Out of Scope

  • Native mobile app
  • Physical shipping/logistics management
  • Cryptocurrency / blockchain payments
  • AI recommendation engine
  • Multi-language / multi-currency support
  • Third-party seller API integrations
