Project Scope

Focused Phase 1 scope with clear boundaries

06
✅

In Scope

  • Responsive web marketplace
  • Product and material swap system
  • Creator profiles and portfolios
  • Secure payment gateway
  • Order management and tracking
  • Reviews and ratings
  • Forums and direct messaging
  • Custom order requests
  • Admin dashboard and moderation
  • Seller analytics dashboard
  • Email alerts and notifications
  • Support ticketing system
🚫

Out of Scope for Phase 1

  • Native iOS / Android mobile application.
  • Physical shipping or logistics management.
  • Cryptocurrency or blockchain-based payments.
  • AI-powered product recommendation engine.
  • Multi-language or multi-currency support.
  • Third-party seller API integrations.
Protect the MVP baseline while preserving the features that create differentiation: swap, community, identity, and trust.

Risks & Mitigation Overview

Key risks are significant but controllable

10

Top Project Risks

Risk L / I Mitigation Strategy
Low initial user adoption Medium / High Pre-launch campaign, 100 founding artisans, referral program.
Payment fraud or breach Low / Very High PCI-DSS gateway, encryption, audits, penetration testing.
Competition from Etsy / Amazon High / Medium Differentiate by exchange system, lower fees, and community focus.
Exchange system abuse Medium / Medium Verification, escrow-style exchange, moderation, dispute process.
Scope creep Medium / High Agile sprint goals, MVP baseline, formal change control.
Break-even delay Medium / High Monthly revenue/OPEX review, pricing adjustment, CAC control.

Mitigation Priorities

📉
Adoption Risk
Build waitlist, launch beta campaign, recruit founding artisans, and activate referrals.
🔐
Security Risk
Use PCI-DSS payments, SSL, RBAC, monitoring, audits, and penetration testing.
🧩
Scope Risk
Freeze MVP baseline and require sponsor approval for material scope changes.
⚖️
Legal Risk
Prepare GDPR, tax, privacy policy, terms of service, and e-commerce compliance early.
🛡️
Exchange Abuse
Add verification, exchange rules, moderation, and dispute-resolution workflow.
💸
Financial Risk
Track revenue, OPEX, CAC, take rate, and LTV/CAC monthly to protect Year 2 break-even.
Risk approach: build liquidity before launch, protect MVP scope, secure payment and data flows, prepare compliance early, and monitor financial performance after launch.
